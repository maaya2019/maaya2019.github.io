Battle of Bands

A symphonic contest between bands that either let you drift into a trance or get you up on your feet with your headbanging. All in all, a string of super energetic performances that leaves you screaming “Encore, encore!”

Rules

  1. All bands from professional colleges are allowed regardless of age.
  2. Any changes need to be intimated to the event organisers a day beforehand.
  3. Professional conduct on stage must be ensured.
  4. Vulgarity in any form will not be entertained whatsoever.
  5. No individual can perform with more than one participating team.
  6. All bands must be present at the venue of the event atleast 30 mins prior to the event.
  7. Only registered band members can perform on stage.
  8. Slot picking will be be held before the event at the venue.
  9. Time limit: 12+2(inclusive of soundcheck).
  10. 5% of total marks will be deducted for each minute exceeded beyond the time limit.
  11. No pre-recorded sounds or loops allowed.
  12. Bring your own instruments, drum kit will be provided.
  13. If bands have any specific equipment requirements, it must be intimated to the event organisers a week beforehand.
  14. Original compositions are encouraged. Lyrics of the same if any, must be sent to the event organisers atleast a day beforehand.
  15. All genres and languages are entertained.
  16. Organisers will not be held responsible for loss of band equipment or possessions.
  17. Valid ID card is mandatory.
  18. Judges' as well as event organisers' decisions are final.
  19. Not adhering to any of the above regulations will lead to immediate disqualification of the team in concern.

  2. No on-spot registrations.
  3. Team Size: 5-15 performers.
  4. Time limit: 4-6 min (+1 min setup time) this is not to be exceeded.
  5. Judging Criteria: Choreography, Energy, Synchronization, Stage utilization, Costumes, Innovation, Overall impact.
  6. Usage of props is allowed. These have to be brought by the teams themselves but does not carry any extra marks. (No dangerous props of any kind of fluid or flame)
  7. No solo performance is allowed at any point of time.
  8. Live music is not allowed.
  9. Pre-recorded music should be sent as a mail and brought in a pen drive in .mp3 format only.
  10. Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.

Acapella / Western Acoustics

Sung without an instrumental accompaniment, this event brings out the Pentatonix in you. An event that is sure to get your feet tapping and head swaying, leaving you with a golly perspective of a familiar tune.

Rules

  1. For Acapella: 12 participants/team For Acoustic: 8 participants/team
  2. Each team has 2+8 minutes exceeding which will bring down 5% of the score per minute.
  3. Participating teams must declare themselves as either Acoustic or Acapella prior to the event.
  4. Only acoustic instruments and electric bass are allowed. Keyboard to be used only in grand piano mode.
  5. Teams are not allowed to use any backing tracks or pre-recorded sounds or loops.
  6. Teams must maintain a professional conduct.
  7. No participant can be a part of 2 different teams for the same event.
  8. Teams will be judged on the basis of: creativity, harmony, pitch, sync, stage presence, group dynamics.
  9. Original Compositions are encouraged.
  10. Lyrics of the Original Composition need to be sent to the event organizers at least 2 days prior to the competiton via email.
  11. Specific requirements must be informed to the event organizers well beforehand the day of the event.
  12. The decisions made by the judge will be final.
  13. All participating teams need to assemble at least 30 minutes prior to the venue of the event.
  14. Please make sure that each team member has an ID card.
  15. Team(s) that fail to meet these rules will be disqualified immediately.
