Debate

Rules

  1. 2 people in a team.
  2. Four topics will be mentioned or given to the participants after they have registered.
  3. The topic and the motion (for or against) will be decided on the spot for every team.
  4. An introductory speech of 3 mins is compulsory (as a team). A warning sign will be given by the moderator. Speaking prior the given time would lead to disqualification of the team.
  5. After the speech from both the sides, there will be an interrogation round where each team gets a chance to ask their opponent team questions. Participants would get limited time to answer these questions. Speaking prior the given time would lead to disqualification of the team.
  6. No abusive language allowed.
  7. No personal and communal comments will be tolerated.
  8. Judge’s decision is final.

JAM

Rules

  1. 2-3 members per team. Every member must speak in atleast one of the rounds.
  2. The JAM master is god. No questions or arguments once the final decision has been made.
  3. Abusive/inappropriate terms and comments will lead to immediate disqualification.
  4. There will be 4 rounds of elimination before the final.
  5. The topics will be given 30 secs prior to one's turn after which a bell will indicate the start of one minute.
  6. Panelists can be challenged only after pressing the buzzer, and on the JAM master's consent.
  7. Usage of any other language except English is prohibited.
