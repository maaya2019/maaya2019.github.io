Photography

Rules

  1. Individual event
  2. Only Digital cameras are to be used during the event
  3. Participants must bring their own digital cameras.
  4. Memory card will be cleared at the start of the event.
  5. Participants will be given a time slot of 4 hours after the event to get their shots.
  6. Only basic editing is allowed
  7. Finally, EXIF data should be present in the metadata of submitted photos.

Mask It

Rules

  1. It is a team event. There can be only 2 participants in a team.
  2. Participants should bring their own colours, brushes, etc.
  3. Time limit is 60 minutes.
  4. Topic will be given on spot.
  5. No references will be allowed.
  6. Prosthetic pieces are not allowed.
  7. Stencils and stamps of any kind are not allowed.
  8. Use of toxic paints are strictly prohibited.
  9. Participants will be judged based on their innovation, creativity and design.
  10. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Sketching

Rules

  1. Individual event
  2. Participants will be required to bring their own pencils(colour or otherwise). Sketching paper will be provided.
  3. The topic will be intimated on the day of the event.
  4. Usage of watercolour or any similar paint is strictly prohibited.
  5. Participants will be given a general topic and will be assessed for the same. Decision of the judges will be final.
