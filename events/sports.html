Street Basketball (Men)

Rules

  1. The Tournament will be held as per the latest FIBA rules for men’s and women’s teams.
  2. Players and teams fielding unregistered players will result in disqualification of the team.
  3. All teams must report to the event venue half an hour prior to the start of the event.
  4. No requests for preponement or postponement of matches will be entertained.
    5. However, the organisers will have the right to alter, prepone/postpone any match.
  5. No protest on referees’ decision will be entertained.
  6. The matches will be 3v3 half court.
  7. Each team can have up to 4 players (i.e. 3 on court + 1 substitute) and a minimum of 3 players.
  8. If a player in a 3 man team is injured/cannot continue, the team will have to forfeit the match, no changes in the teams is allowed after the registration is complete.
  9. Since it is a half court match, the rule of “all-clear” will apply during the course of the match.

Street Football

Rules

  1. The team will consist of 5 players and 3 rolling subs (5+3)
  2. All matches played will be knockout games
  3. The total duration of the game will be 10 minutes.
  4. Teams are required to be present 30 minutes prior to start of the event, no alteration of the schedule will be done.
  5. No fights will be entertained, the referee’s decision will be final.
  6. If any cards received during the match, yellow card means the player is out for 2 minutes until he can come back onto the field and red cards means the player will out for that particular match and the proceeding match if the team advances to the next round.
  7. If a team receives 2 red cards in the same game, the match will be forfeited and the opponent team will win.
  8. Every player participating in a team needs to have a college ID and should submit it to the organisers prior to the start of the event, if any player/team doesn’t do the same, they will not be allowed to play
  9. No player can play for 2 different teams.
  10. The organisers decision wrt to the event is final.
    11. Note: Bibs won’t be provided, so it would be ideal if a team could wear one colour.

Street Cricket

Rules

  1. 5 players on the field
  2. 5 overs per innings.
  3. Each player has to bowl one over.
  4. A bowler cannot bend his knees while bowling.
  5. Runs can be scored by hitting 4's, running between wickets and extras.
  6. A batsman can be declared out in these cases - Bowled, caught out, stumped, runout, hit wicket,hitting the ball directly over the ropes.
  7. The captain of the team will be responsible for the actions of all his team members.
  8. In case of a tie, the result will be decided by a super over.
  9. During the tournament all participants are expected to behave professionally and should avoid abusive language/gestures/ question umpires decisions.
  10. For every innings 5th over will be jackpot over (Runs scored by batting team will get doubled and if a wicket falls 4 runs will be deducted from batting team's total)
  11. Tournament will be in a knockout format.
  12. No second chance will be given to any knocked out team on any grounds.
  13. If any team found indulging in malpractices, unfair means, arguing and fighting with umpires or scorers, tampering with any sports equipment or fighting with opponents, they will be immediately disqualified.
