FIFA

Rules

  1. The FIFA tournament is an individual event.
  2. All matches played will be knockout games.
  3. Matches will be played over 2 legs. Away goals rules apply. That is, in case of equal number of goals after both legs, the player who scored more away goals will be declared winner. In case of a tie, winner will be decided by penalties.
  4. Half time duration will be 5 minutes.
  5. Any team and formation can be chosen by the players. Women's teams can be chosen, provided both players consent to it. Custom team building is NOT allowed.
  6. Formations, tactics, and strategies can be changed in between matches as per player's wish, while the player has possession of the ball, after prior notice.
  7. Players can bring their own controllers or keyboard (and mouse) if they want to. Controllers will be provided in case players don't have their own. Any damage caused to the controllers provided will be charged to the player to whom the said controller was assigned.

Counter Strike

Rules

  1. 5 players in one team
  2. Need to bring your own laptop, charger, mouse, headphones etc. In case of any last minute problems, at most one laptop can be provided by the organisers.
  3. Game will be played on Counter Strike GO(Global Offensive).
  4. Selection of terrorist and counter terrorist wil be done by a knife round or toss of coin. The winning team gets to choose the side.
  5. Team to reach 7 wins first will be the winner of the match (for Knockout matches).
  6. The starting rounds will be knockout rounds till number of teams is less than or equal to 4.
  7. The last 4 will have a league round and top 2 will be playing finals.
  8. In league matches the team reaching 9 wins first will win the match.
  9. The final match will be a race to 13.
  10. No use of mobiles during matches.
  11. Laptops should have proper wifi connectivity in case the LAN cable is damaged or not available.
  12. Selections of map, starting money and time limit will be done by the organisers.
